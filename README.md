Problem Statement
Literacy rates are a vital measure of a country’s human development, economic growth, 
and educational outreach. In this project, we analyze adult literacy, youth literacy, illiteracy population, GDP, 
and years of schooling data across countries and years. The aim is to uncover patterns, correlations, and disparities in education across the globe.
